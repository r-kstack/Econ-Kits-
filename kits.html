The library

One syllabus topic per kit. Everything a lesson needs.

Every kit follows the same structure: a plain-language teacher guide, slide decks, student worksheets, fully worked solutions, and auto-marked quizzes. Kits are mapped to the NSW HSC Economics syllabus and built to be taught by teachers without an economics background.
